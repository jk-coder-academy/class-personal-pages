Past

Until recently i was working as an airline pilot, based in Hong Kong. I started in Hong Kong in 2010, and flew both 747 and 777 aircraft.

The global pandemic has decimated the airline industry, and I flew very little through 2020, and when i did fly, it was cargo only. Usually the cargo was loaded in the cargo hold and we flew witih an empty cabin, but some aircraft had been modified to carry cargo on the main deck instead of seating. The last time i saw a passenger on my flight was early 2020.
